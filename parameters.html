This section includes the specified design values for parameters and constants used in the Connected Device. A tolerance is specified to support appropriate measurements during testing.

Voltage and Current#

Name Value Tolerance
Battery Voltage - ± 100 mV
LOW_VOLTAGE 2.5 V ± 100 mV
VERY_LOW_VOLTAGE 2.3 V ± 100 mV
BROWN_OUT_VOLTAGE 1.8 V ± 100 mV
SHUTDOWN_MODE_CURRENT 230 nA ± 50 nA (DOSE BUTTON not pressed)
-50 nA / +3 uA (DOSE BUTTON pressed)

The battery voltage measurement accuracy is specified in the microcontroller data sheet as 13 mV with a resolution of 50 mV for a reference design operating at 3.0 V and 25 °C. Additional tolerance is specified to allow for variations in operating temperature and microcontroller supply voltage.

The SHUTDOWN_MODE_CURRENT value is based upon the power consumption of the microcontroller in Shutdown Mode, the RTC and leakage current. It is also the steady state value: the current will be higher in the few minutes immediately after the device is powered. Tolerances are applied to account for fluctuations in temperature and the charge state of supporting components. Due to its very low value and volatility of the instantaneous value due to noise, it is expected that tests of SHUTDOWN_MODE_CURRENT will be averaged over a small number of seconds.

If the Dose Button is held after the Connected Device enters Shutdown Mode, the effects of higher leakage current require a higher tolerance to be applied to SHUTDOWN_MODE_CURRENT.

Temperature#

Name Value Tolerance
Microcontroller temperature - ± 7 °C

The temperature accuracy is specified in the microcontroller data sheet as ± 5 °C for a reference design operating at 3.0 V and 25 °C.

Additional tolerance is specified for variations in operating temperature and microcontroller supply voltage.

Dose and record limits#

Name Value Tolerance
FAST_DOSE_RATE 26 Units per second ± 1 Unit per second
FAST_DOSE_UNITS 6 Units -
RECORD_STORAGE_SIZE Exact number given by UDI field (93). 3228 records for Cynthia devices. -
VERY_FAST_DOSE_RATE 2000 Units per second - 667 Units per second / + 2000 Units per second
VERY_FAST_DOSE_UNITS 2 Units -
DOSE_MEASUREMENT_LIMIT 4000 Units per second -

The tolerance for the FAST_DOSE_RATE is based on the tolerance specified in the product requirements.

The tolerance for the VERY_FAST_DOSE_RATE is provided to take into account the ADC sampling resolution of the microcontroller.

DOSE BUTTON Parameters#

Name Value Tolerance
DOSE_COMPLETE 100 ms - 0 ms / +50 ms
SYNC_TIME 0.5 s ± 50 ms
PAIR_TIME 3 s ± 50 ms
BUTTON_TIMEOUT 20 s ± 100 ms
DOSE_MEASUREMENT_TIMEOUT 60 s ± 100 ms

The tolerances are provided to take into account physical factors, such as the electronic switching point, the RC time constant on the release of the Dose Button and the effects of temperature drift.

Higher tolerances are applied to higher-value parameters in order to accommodate the effects of clock drift due to the longer measurement period involved.

LED Parameters#

Name Value Tolerance
BLE_ACTIVITY_MIN_INDICATION_CYCLES 2 -
BLE_SYNC_INDICATION_CYCLE_TIME 1 s ± 50 ms
BLE_SYNC_INDICATION_DUTY_CYCLE 10 % ± 1 %
BLE_PAIR_INDICATION_CYCLE_TIME 2 s ± 50 ms
BLE_PAIR_INDICATION_DUTY_CYCLE 5 % ± 1 %

The tolerances are provided to take into account physical factors, component value variations and the effects of temperature drift.
