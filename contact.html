  • REPORTED DOSE,
  • FAST DOSE FLAG,
  • VERY FAST DOSE FLAG,
  • BUTTON TIMEOUT FLAG,
  • LOW VOLTAGE FLAG,
  • CHECK VALUE of the DOSE RECORD

A wrapper structure around the latest record produced by the CONNECTED DEVICE, with additional data, including:

  • RECORD NUMBER
